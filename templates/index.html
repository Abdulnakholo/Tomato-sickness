Tomato Sickness

what is tomato sickness detector?
Is your tomato plant showing some signs of illness? Tomato sickness detector has you covered. The web app involves application of deep learning's convolutional neuron network to classify a tomato leaf in a certain class of illness or being healthy.
You are required to take a clear and a close picture of your tomato leaf whith a clear background. The image should clearly show the leaf's detailed features. Upload the image, and then press "Predict Image" to see if your plant is healthy or is suffering from some tomato plant's diseases. You will also get the percentage accuracy of the predicted results and some standard solution to your problem.
